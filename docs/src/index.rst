HKUST HPC Documentation
=======================

Searchable doc at `readthedocs.io <https://hkust-hpc-docs.readthedocs.io/>`_.

**Tutorials, docs and knowledge base** for AI / HPC users using central HPC resources at
the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST).

Despite this doc is tailored for Superpod and HPC4 maintained by Information Technology
Services Center (ITSC), you may find some useful techniques in general and works on
other HPC systems as well.
